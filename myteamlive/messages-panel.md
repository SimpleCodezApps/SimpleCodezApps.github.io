During a broadcast, use the Messages panel to trigger custom messages, Celly Stickers, and branding.

## Basics
1. Pick Home or Away for team-based messages.
2. Override the on-screen duration if needed.
3. Tap a message to display it immediately.

## Using Logo Overlays
Logo overlays are persistent and stay visible in the bottom-right corner until you remove them.

1. Tap a logo overlay in the Messages panel to display it immediately.
2. The logo overlay remains on screen until you tap it again to dismiss it.
3. Only one logo overlay can be active at a time—tapping a new logo overlay replaces the current one.

## Managing Messages
- Create and edit overlays in the [Messages Tab](messages-tab).
- The panel lists only the messages for the current sport and sorts them by name.

## Tips
- Use descriptive names so you can find messages quickly while live.
- Keep durations short for quick announcements.
- Celly Stickers add personality; use them sparingly so they do not cover live action.
- Logo overlays work well for sponsor or team branding that should stay visible throughout the broadcast.
