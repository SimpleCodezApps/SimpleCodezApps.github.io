# Facebook Endpoint Guide

Schedule a Facebook Live event on the web, then stream to it from MyTeamLive.

## Schedule a Live Event
1. On Facebook.com, choose **Live Video**.
2. Select **Create a live video event**.
3. Enter the event name, description, date, time, and privacy.
4. Click **Next**, then open **Settings > Stream**.
5. Toggle **Use a persistent stream key** and copy it for later reuse.
6. Finish by creating the event.

## Configure the Endpoint in MyTeamLive
1. Open the [Live Streams Tab](live-streams).
2. Tap **Add Live Stream**.
3. Choose Facebook as the service to auto-fill the server URL.
4. Paste the stream key from Facebook.
5. Give the destination a recognizable name.
6. Tap **Save**. Persistent keys let you reuse the same entry for future events.

## Go Live
1. Open the [Go Live Tab](go-live).
2. Select the Facebook endpoint.
3. Complete the broadcast setup and tap **Go Live**.
4. Tap Play/Pause to enter Standby. Facebook will switch to Live at the scheduled time.

## End the Broadcast
Stop the stream in the app to end the Facebook Live video.

## FAQs
- Expect a short delay between the app and the viewer feed.
- Facebook Live videos remain available for 30 days.
