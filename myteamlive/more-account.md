Use the Account area under the More tab to view subscription status and manage billing.

## Subscription Management
1. Review the current plan, renewal state, and expiration date.
2. Tap **Subscribe** to start a plan if you are not active yet.
3. Tap **Manage in App Store** to change or cancel an existing plan.
4. The expiration field shows when access renews or ends.
