Use the Saved Recordings section in the More tab to review, share, or remove the local MP4 archives created when you select **Record Locally Only** on the Go Live *Select Live Stream* step.

## Locate Recordings
- Each entry shows `HOME @ AWAY` with the recording date/time and an optional part number (if you stopped and restarted the livestream multiple times).
- A green checkmark indicates the recording has been saved to photos successfully.
- "Combined" appears in the subtitle for a recording created by combining multiple parts.

## Play a Recording
- Tap a recording to start playback.

## Recording Info
- Tap the info icon on a row to see resolution, frame rate, file size, recorded date, and whether it has been saved to Photos.

## Select Multiple Recordings
1. Tap **Edit** in the top left to enter selection mode.
2. Tap recordings to select them, or use **Select All** / **Deselect All**.
3. With recordings selected, use **Save** to copy them all to Photos, **Delete** to remove them all, or **Combine** (enabled once 2 or more are selected) to merge them into a single recording.
4. Tap **Done** to exit selection mode.

## Share a Recording
1. Tap the share icon on the right edge of the row.
2. Pick AirDrop, Messages, Mail, or another destination to send the MP4.

## Save to Photos
1. Swipe left on a recording and tap **Save**.
2. Grant Photos access the first time so the video can be copied into your library.

## Delete a Recording
1. Swipe left on a recording and tap **Delete**.
2. Confirm to remove the MP4 from the device. This cannot be undone.
